About Summer

Summer was born in August 2014, bringing constant joy, silliness, and positivity. She loved all things Disney and taking care of her dogs: Daisy, Buzz, and Lilly. In March 2015, at just seven months old, she was diagnosed with a rare Malignant Rhabdoid Tumor. For over a year and a half, she bravely faced high-intensity treatments, never letting cancer define her. She remains our absolute hero.
