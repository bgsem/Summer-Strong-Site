Why We Play

The Summer Strong Hero Fund was established to create a legacy of hope. Every year, we gather on the green not just to play golf, but to rally our community around a critical cause: funding life-saving research for childhood cancer through Alex's Lemonade Stand Foundation.

Since our inception, our incredible supporters have helped raise over $534,000. When you register for a foursome, sponsor a hole, or make a donation, you are directly funding breakthrough treatments and bringing us closer to a cure.

"When life gives you lemons, play golf and make a difference."
